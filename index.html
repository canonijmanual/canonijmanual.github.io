Simplest Way to Install Canon Printer Setup

The simplest way to install your Canon printer depends on whether you have the original Setup CD or not:


With Setup CD:

  1. Insert the CD into your computer's CD drive.
  2. The installation wizard should automatically launch.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions. This will typically involve selecting your language, connecting your printer (usually with a USB cable when prompted), and installing the necessary software.

Without Setup CD:

  1. Visit the Canon USA website "canon.com".
  2. Click on Support.
  3. Enter your printer model number in the search bar.
  4. Go to the Drivers & Downloads section.
  5. Download the latest driver software for your operating system (Windows, Mac, etc.).
  6. Run the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions for installation.

How to Download and Install Canon Printer Driver?

To download and install a Canon printer driver, follow these steps:

  • Go to the Canon website "Canon ij setup" and click on "Support".
  • Select "Printers" and enter your printer model number or select it from the list.
  • Click on "Downloads" and select the operating system (Windows or Mac).
  • Choose the driver language and click "Download".
  • Run the downloaded file (it should be named "cnmjXXXX.exe" or "cnmjXXXX.dmg") and follow the installation prompts.
  • Connect the printer to your computer or network.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

Steps to Connect Canon IJ Printer Setup with Wireless Network

Following are the steps to connect a Canon IJ printer to a wireless network:

  • Make sure the printer is turned on and in range of the wireless network.
  • Press and hold the Wi-Fi button on the printer until the Wi-Fi light starts flashing.
  • Open the Canon PRINT Inkjet/SELPHY app on your smartphone or tablet.
  • Tap "Add Printer" and select your printer model.
  • Follow the prompts to connect the printer to your wireless network.
  • Enter your Wi-Fi network password (WEP/WPA/WPA2).
  • Wait for the printer to connect to the network (may take a few minutes).
  • Once connected, you can print wirelessly from your device.
